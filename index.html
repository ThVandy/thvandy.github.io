My Story

My journey began at seven, when a simple Lego robot sparked a lifelong passion. That very first creation—an optical path‑detection robot—was more than child’s play; it foreshadowed a future driven by innovation. From that moment on, robotics and programming weren’t just hobbies—they were my true calling.

In college, I enrolled in business studies, only to discover that my heart belonged in tech. I switched to application development, immersing myself in code and problem‑solving. Though financial obstacles prevented me from finishing my degree, I carried forward every lesson and insight I gained.

Balancing long workdays, bills, and the responsibilities of parenting, I never stopped building. I explored tools like Unity and Fusion 360, created several game prototypes, and engineered functional hardware—including a custom, API‑controlled webcam mounted on a 3D‑printed, motorized base. Project by project, line by line, I advanced my craft and pressed onward.

Today, I’m not merely working—I’m actively designing a future powered by code, creativity, and perseverance. That first Lego robot was just the beginning.
